Privacy Policy for WaterWink

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WaterWink handles your information. WaterWink is a simple daily water reminder and hydration tracking app designed to help users remember to drink water and track daily water intake.

Important: WaterWink does not require an account, does not sell your personal data, and stores app data locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

WaterWink may ask you to enter basic information to personalize your hydration reminders and daily water goal. This may include:

  • Your name or nickname
  • Your weight
  • Your daily water goal
  • Your wake-up and sleep time
  • Your reminder interval preferences
  • Your daily water intake history

This information is used only inside the app to provide hydration tracking and reminder features.

2. How Your Information Is Stored

WaterWink stores your information locally on your device. We do not upload your hydration data to any external server. Your data remains on your device unless you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you enter is used to:

  • Calculate or manage your daily water goal
  • Track your daily water intake
  • Show hydration progress and history
  • Schedule water reminder notifications
  • Improve your in-app experience

4. Notifications

WaterWink may request notification permission to send water reminder notifications. These notifications are used only to remind you to drink water based on your selected schedule and preferences.

You can enable or disable notifications at any time from the app settings or from your device settings.

5. Permissions Used

WaterWink may use the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send water reminder alerts.
  • Vibration: To provide vibration feedback for reminders if enabled.

WaterWink does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, or files.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. WaterWink does not use your data for advertising purposes.

7. Third-Party Services

WaterWink does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. If third-party services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your app data by using the clear data option inside the app, clearing the app storage from your device settings, or uninstalling the app.

9. Children’s Privacy

WaterWink is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us so we can help remove it where possible.

10. Health Disclaimer

WaterWink provides general hydration reminders only. It is not medical advice and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical guidance. If you have a medical condition or specific hydration requirements, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

11. Security

Since WaterWink stores data locally on your device, your data security also depends on your device security. We recommend keeping your device protected with a password, PIN, fingerprint, or other security method.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
